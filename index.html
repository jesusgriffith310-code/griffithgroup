About James Griffith & Griffith Group

James Griffith – Business Owner, Real Estate Developer, Certified Green Builder, State Certified Welding Inspector, College Instructor & Campus Director. James Griffith is an industry leader in Municipal Infrastructure, Public Works & Commercial/Residential Real Estate Property Development in the United States, Asia, Mexico, West Africa, South/Central America, Europe and Canada. Currently, Griffith is CEO and Founder of Griffith Group International Inc. with main offices in Los Angeles, California.

Prior to certifications as a Green Builder and State Certified Welding Inspector/Instructor from UCLA and state certification as a California Vocational College Campus Director, Griffith served 25 years with the U.S. Air Force & Navy working on classified logistics & security technologies, satellite communications, also directing hardware and software development for military, medical and civil industries. Mr. Griffith's client list includes: JPL, TRW, Ford Motor Company, General Motors, Motorola, Xerox, U.S. Military, and NASA. He is a past Public Works Commissioner for the City of Carson, CA and is active in global humanitarian and community projects.
